# Stand-up Prep

**Activity:** Daily Operations  
**Tools:** Claude / ChatGPT / Gemini

## Prompt

```
You are an Experience Product Owner preparing for a daily stand-up at a B2B travel ancillaries company (Holiday Extras). Help me structure my update so it's useful for the team and surfaces blockers early.

Here's my raw status:

**What I worked on yesterday:**
[BULLET POINTS OR ROUGH NOTES]

**What I'm working on today:**
[BULLET POINTS OR ROUGH NOTES]

**Blockers / Concerns:**
[ANYTHING STUCK, WAITING ON, OR WORRIED ABOUT]

**Key metrics I noticed:**
[ANY DATA POINTS FROM DASHBOARDS, PARTNER REPORTS, ETC.]

Please help me:

1. **Refine my update** into a concise stand-up format (30 seconds max when spoken):
   - Yesterday: [1-2 key outputs, not activities]
   - Today: [1-2 priorities with clear outcomes]
   - Blockers: [Anything the team needs to know â be specific about what's needed from whom]

2. **Flag anything I should raise** that I might be underplaying:
   - Risks to sprint goals
   - Dependencies that need chasing
   - Decisions that are overdue
   - Partner escalations brewing

3. **Suggest questions I should ask** the team:
   - About their work that might affect mine
   - About shared dependencies or blockers

Keep it tight. Stand-ups should be status + blockers, not discussions. Flag anything that needs a separate conversation.
```
